Our Story

A Brooklyn tradition,
fresh every morning.

Brooklyn Bagels & Deli has been a neighborhood staple for decades — a place where the coffee is always hot, the bagels are always fresh, and familiar faces are always welcome. We bake everything from scratch each morning so that every bite you take is made with care.

Hand-rolled bagels baked in-house each morning before sunrise.
Boar's Head meats & cheeses on every deli order, no exceptions.
Fast, friendly service — because your time matters as much as your meal.
Full catering for your next meeting, party, or gathering.
